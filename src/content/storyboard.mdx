<Screen id="how-it-works" title="How it works">
  <TopBar title="How it works" showBack />
  <Text h1>MDX is the language</Text>
  <Text note="No imports in MDX — components are registered globally.">
    Author specs in src/content/*.mdx with YAML frontmatter title. Save a file and codegen extracts screens, validates links, and regenerates routes.
  </Text>

    <Text h3>Example</Text>

<Screen id="philosophy" title="Philosophy">
  <TopBar title="Philosophy" showBack />
  <Text h1>Intent over appearance</Text>
  <Text>Storyboard is not Figma. Primitives render as structural wireframes — borders and semantic HTML, not brand design.</Text>
  <Text h3>Storybook for UX flows</Text>
  <Text>Designers decide how something looks. Storyboard specifies what exists and how users move through it.</Text>
  <Container border danger>
    <Text h4>Non-goals</Text>
    <Text>Pixel-perfect mockups, rich styling, animations, or design tooling</Text>
  </Container>
